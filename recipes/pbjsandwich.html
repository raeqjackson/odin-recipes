Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Just a simple pb&j for peanut butter lovers

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 jar of jelly

Steps

  1. Take the 2 pieces of bread and spread peanut butter on one side of each slice.
  2. Take one of the pieces of bread and spread jelly on the side of bread that has peanut butter.
  3. Cut the sandwich in half, if you would like to. Enjoy!
