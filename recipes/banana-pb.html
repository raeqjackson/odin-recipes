Banana and Peanut Butter

Description

Just simple mashed banana and peanut butter

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and put it in a bowl.
  2. Mash the banana up until you like the consistency.
  3. Add the peanut butter. Continue to mash the banana and peanut butter together until they are mixed thoroughly. Enjoy!
