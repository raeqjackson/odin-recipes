Pizza

Description

Just a simple pepperoni pizza anyone can make!

Ingredients

  • 1 Prepared Pizza Dough (size of your choice)
  • 1 can of pizza sauce
  • 1 bag of shredded cheese of your choice
  • 1 bag of pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to pizza dough instructions. Grease a pan or place nonstick foil in pan.
  2. Place pizza dough in pan.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce around the crust leaving 1inch of crust.
  4. Scatter shredded cheese all over pizza. Make sure to brush off any cheese on the crust. This ensures that the cheese doesn't burn on the crust.
  5. Place pepperoni on top of the cheese.
  6. Put the pizza in the oven and set a timer based on the pizza dough instructions. And wait!
